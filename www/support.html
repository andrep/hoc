HOC: A Haskell to Objective-C Binding

Mailing Lists

There are four mailing lists where you can contact the HOC developers and other users:

hoc-announce@lists.sourceforge.net   Announcements of HOC releases and related tools (low-traffic)
hoc-users@lists.sourceforge.net General HOC discussions
hoc-devel@lists.sourceforge.net HOC developer & implementation discussions
hoc-cvs@lists.sourceforge.net CVS commit log messages
